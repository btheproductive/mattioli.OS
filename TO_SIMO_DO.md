# TO_SIMO_DO

## Manual Actions Required
1.  **Verify Statistics Colors**:
    -   Open the application in the browser.
    -   Navigate to the "Stats" page.
    -   Select a specific goal/habit from the dropdown.
    -   Verify that:
        -   Completed days in the "Trend Ultimi 30 Giorni" chart are **Green**.
        -   Missed days in the chart are **Red**.
        -   Completed days in the "Calendario Annuale" are **Green**.
        -   Missed days in the calendar are **Red**.
        -   High performance bars in "Performance per Giorno" are **Green**.
        -   Legends correctly reflect these colors.
